Hi!

I am so excited that you are interested in my art and want to find out more about me! I graduated from the Yerevan State Academy of Fine Arts, majoring in Graphic Arts. Afterwards, I worked at the Military Museum of Armenia as an Artist and a Graphic Designer. If you visit Armenia one day, you can see my works displayed in the museum. I immigrated from Armenia to New York in 2008.

I love, love, love drawing portraits! I have an Etsy shop MariaPencilArt, where you can order a custom portrait drawn by me from a photograph. Alternatively, you can contact me through my website for commissioned work.
